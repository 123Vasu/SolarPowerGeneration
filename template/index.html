About Solar Power Prediction

The Solar Power Generation Project is a machine learning-based project aimed at predicting the solar power generation for a given location based on historical weather data. The project uses data from the Kaggle to train a Random Forest Regression model, which is then used to make predictions.

The main objective of the Solar Power Generation Project is to provide accurate predictions of solar power generation for a given location, which can be useful for various stakeholders in the renewable energy sector. The project can help solar power plant operators optimize their energy production, and can also help energy analysts and policymakers make informed decisions about the future of renewable energy.

In the future, we plan to expand the scope of the Solar Power Generation Project by including data from other sources, such as satellite imagery and social media sentiment analysis. We also plan to develop a web application that allows users to input their location and receive real-time predictions of solar power generation.
